Story

Built for vinyl nights and second cups.

Hilltop started as a weekend pop-up in Northlake, TX. We kept the paisley bandana from the first bake sale and still wrap our loaves in it today. Everything is made small-batch, sourced locally, and served with friendly hospitality.
