Since 2016

Built for Slow Mornings and Good Conversation.

Hilltop Cafe started as a tiny corner coffee bar and grew into a community gathering spot. We source local ingredients, roast in small batches, and keep things simple: quality food, kind service, and space to breathe.

"Our goal is to be your favorite part of the day - whether you are grabbing a quick cortado or settling in for brunch."
