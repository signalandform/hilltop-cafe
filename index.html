Story

Built for slow mornings, vinyl nights, and second cups.

Hilltop started as a weekend pop-up on Summit Lane. We kept the paisley bandana from the first bake sale and still wrap our loaves in it today. Everything is made small-batch, sourced locally, and served with friendly hospitality.

Beans Single-origin rotating
